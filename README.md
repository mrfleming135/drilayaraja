## About Dr. K. Ilayaraja

Dr. K. Ilayaraja is a physician and social impact leader dedicated to improving healthcare accessibility in rural and underserved regions. His work focuses on bridging the gap between urban medical infrastructure and remote populations.

In addition to healthcare, he is deeply committed to educational development and established the **Intensive Educational Care Unit (IE CARE)** framework to support slow learners through structured academic intervention.

In 2018, he founded the **Dr. Ilayaraja Education and Health Services Trust**, which focuses on expanding healthcare access and educational opportunities for underserved communities.

The trust is inspired by the **PURA (Providing Urban Amenities in Rural Areas)** vision proposed by **Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam**, which emphasizes sustainable rural development through improved infrastructure and services.

---

## Vision

To transform rural regions by developing urban-level infrastructure and services, creating economic opportunities, attracting talent, and fostering vibrant rural communities.

## Mission

To collaborate with government and non-government organisations to build self-sustaining rural communities by expanding access to quality education and comprehensive healthcare services.
