# Dr. Ilayaraja Education and Health Services Trust

Official website for **Dr. Ilayaraja Education and Health Services Trust**, an initiative dedicated to improving healthcare access and educational opportunities for underserved communities.

## About the Trust

Founded in 2018 by **Dr. K. Ilayaraja**, the trust focuses on two important areas:

- Accessible healthcare for rural communities
- Inclusive education for slow learners and underserved students

The organisation works to bridge the gap between urban resources and remote populations through community outreach, education support, and medical initiatives.

## Key Initiatives

### Healthcare Access
Providing medical support and healthcare access to remote and underserved regions.

### Intensive Educational Care Unit (IE CARE)
A specialised learning framework designed to help slow learners build strong foundational academic skills.

### Community Development
Supporting communities from fishing and farming backgrounds by improving educational standards and healthcare awareness.
