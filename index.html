About Me

I am a medical professional dedicated to ensuring 24×7 accessible healthcare for underserved rural communities, including a geographically isolated island region bordering Sri Lanka. My work focuses on bridging the gap between urban medical infrastructure and remote populations by mobilising expert healthcare professionals to areas where access is limited.

Beyond healthcare, I am equally committed to inclusive education. I established the Intensive Educational Care Unit (IE CARE) framework to support slow learners in mastering foundational concepts through structured, focused academic intervention. Through our institution, we ensure that students from fishing and farming communities receive education on par with urban standards, creating equal opportunities for long-term growth.

In 2018, I founded the Dr. Ilayaraja Education and Health Services Trust with the belief that education and healthcare are fundamental rights. The trust works to support underserved communities through initiatives that improve learning opportunities and expand access to healthcare services. With the support of partners and dedicated volunteers, our programs continue to positively impact individuals and strengthen communities.The trust is inspired by the PURA (Providing Urban Amenities in Rural Areas) model envisioned by Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam. which focuses on transforming rural regions through improved infrastructure, services, and sustainable development.

My work remains guided by three core principles: Accessibility, Equity and Sustainable Community Development.

Vision

To transform rural regions by developing urban-level infrastructure and services, creating economic opportunities, attracting new talent, and fostering a prosperous and vibrant rural society.

Mission

To collaborate with government and non-government organizations to build self-sustaining rural communities where people can thrive. Our focus is on expanding access to quality education and comprehensive healthcare services in rural areas, ensuring long-term social and economic upliftment.
